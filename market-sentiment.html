What Is Market Sentiment?

Market sentiment is the public mood around a market, asset, instrument, or theme. It can be bullish, bearish, mixed, or neutral depending on what public information is emphasizing.

Sentiment is useful because markets often react not only to facts, but also to expectations, fear, confidence, positioning, headlines, and risk appetite.

Public Sentiment Dash organizes this information so users can compare the public narrative with technical direction and their own market judgment.
