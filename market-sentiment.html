How to Think About It

What Public Sentiment Can Help With

Market context: sentiment helps show whether public narratives are leaning bullish, bearish, mixed, or uncertain.
Instrument comparison: compare indexes, forex, crypto, commodities, bonds, and macro themes in one place.
Headline awareness: see which stories are driving attention across major public financial sources.
Risk awareness: strong public sentiment can sometimes mean crowding, overreaction, or fast changes in expectations.
