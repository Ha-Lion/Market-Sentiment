How the dashboard works

Public Sentiment Dash turns large public headline flow into a cleaner market-sentiment view.

1

Public source collection

The engine scans configured public financial, macro, commodity, forex, crypto, and market news sources.

2

Headline filtering

Collected headlines are filtered and tagged by markets, instruments, and general relevance so the dashboard stays focused.

3

Sentiment grouping

The engine scores the public narrative behind the headlines and labels it as bullish, bearish, mixed, or neutral when enough context exists.

4

Source votes and market focus

The site shows source-level votes, market focus, headline counts, and relevant news to help users understand what public media is emphasizing.

5

Dashboard display

Results are shown on the home page, dashboard page, and news page in a simpler format for quick review.
