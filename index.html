How to Read This Dashboard

What does the 0–100 public sentiment score mean?

The score converts public source votes and headline tone into a fast reading. Lower scores lean bearish, mid-range scores are mixed or neutral, and higher scores lean bullish.

Is this a trade signal?

No. This is a public narrative and sentiment tool. Always confirm with price action, technical analysis, macro data, risk controls, and your own judgment.

What does the comparison tool measure?

It compares selected instruments using headline sentiment, relevant headline count, and technical direction data when available.
