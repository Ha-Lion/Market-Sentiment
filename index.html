Free Global Market Public Sentiment Dashboard

PublicSentimentDash is an AI-built public sentiment dashboard designed to help traders and investors quickly understand what major financial media and public market sources are emphasizing across indexes, futures, bonds, forex, crypto, commodities, and macro themes.

The dashboard continuously scans well-known financial sources, then organizes headlines into a cleaner public-sentiment view with source votes, top market focus, relevant headlines, and technical direction.

Traders typically look at three major layers: technical analysis, fundamental analysis, and public sentiment. This dashboard focuses on the public-sentiment layer and helps show where public narrative may be shifting.
