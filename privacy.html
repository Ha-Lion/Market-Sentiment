Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2026

Public Sentiment Dash respects user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how this website may collect, use, and protect information when visitors use the site.

This site is informational and educational only. It does not provide financial advice, investment advice, trading advice, or personalized recommendations.

Information We May Collect

Public Sentiment Dash may collect limited information in the following ways:

  • Website analytics: Basic usage data such as page views, browser type, device type, approximate location, traffic source, and general engagement may be collected through analytics tools.
  • Anonymous voting: When users vote on market sentiment, the site may store the selected instrument, vote direction, date, and an anonymous browser-based voter identifier.
  • Contact form information: If you submit a message through the contact page, the information you provide may be used to respond to your message.
  • Browser storage: The site may use local storage or similar browser technology to support anonymous voting and basic site functionality.
  • Hosting and security logs: Hosting providers may process standard technical information such as IP address, user agent, request time, and page requests for reliability and security.

How Information Is Used

Information may be used to:

  • Operate and improve the website.
  • Display aggregated anonymous voting results.
  • Understand general traffic and usage trends.
  • Improve content, dashboard quality, and user experience over time.
  • Respond to contact form submissions.
  • Protect the site from abuse, spam, or technical issues.

Anonymous Voting

The voting feature is designed to be anonymous. Votes are used to show aggregated public sentiment. The site does not require account registration to vote.

A browser-based identifier may be stored locally to reduce duplicate voting and help maintain vote quality. Clearing browser storage may remove this identifier.

Third-Party Services

Public Sentiment Dash may use third-party services for hosting, analytics, forms, database functionality, and public content delivery. These may include services such as GitHub Pages, Google Analytics, Supabase, Formspree, and similar tools.

Third-party websites linked from this site have their own privacy practices. Public Sentiment Dash is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of external websites.

Cookies and Local Storage

The site may use cookies, local storage, or similar technologies for analytics, voting, performance, and basic site functionality. Users can control cookies and storage through their browser settings.

Data Sharing

Public Sentiment Dash does not sell personal information. Limited information may be processed by third-party service providers as needed to operate the site.

Financial and Market Data

The site organizes public financial news, technical direction, public sentiment, and anonymous user voting. This information may be incomplete, delayed, inaccurate, or change quickly. Users should not rely on the site as the sole basis for financial decisions.

Children’s Privacy

This site is not intended for children. Public Sentiment Dash does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

You may disable cookies, clear local storage, or use browser privacy controls. If you contact the site directly, you may request that your message-related information be deleted where reasonably possible.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time as the site grows, improves, or adds new features. Updates will be posted on this page.
