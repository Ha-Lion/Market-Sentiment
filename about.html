About the project

About PublicSentimentDash

PublicSentimentDash is a free global market public sentiment dashboard built to help traders, investors, and market watchers quickly understand what public financial coverage is emphasizing across major assets.

The goal is simple: organize noisy financial headlines into a cleaner view of public narrative, source votes, market focus, relevant headlines, and technical direction.

Open Interactive Dashboard Search News & Articles

What the site does

It collects public market headlines, groups them into relevant market buckets, and summarizes the public narrative into a simpler dashboard format.

Who it is for

It is designed for traders, investors, researchers, and anyone who wants a fast read on public market discussion without manually scanning many sources one by one.

What it is not

It is not a trade signal service, not financial advice, and not a substitute for doing technical, macro, or risk analysis on your own.
