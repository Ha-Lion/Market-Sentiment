About PublicSentimentDash

PublicSentimentDash is a market-awareness tool built to help traders, investors, and market watchers quickly understand where public financial narrative is leaning.

The dashboard focuses mainly on the public sentiment layer, while also showing technical direction and helping users place sentiment within broader fundamental market context.

Why this website exists

Markets are noisy. Every day, traders and investors are hit with headlines about stocks, bonds, currencies, commodities, crypto, inflation, central banks, jobs, earnings, and geopolitics.

PublicSentimentDash organizes that public information into a cleaner view so users can quickly see what the financial media is emphasizing and how the overall tone is leaning.

The three major market layers

1) Public Sentiment

Public sentiment shows what the market conversation is focused on. It reflects tone, attention, fear, optimism, and narrative pressure.

2) Fundamental Analysis

Fundamentals look at the deeper reality behind a market: rates, earnings, inflation, jobs, growth, liquidity, supply, demand, and policy.

3) Technical Analysis

Technical analysis looks at price behavior: trend, momentum, moving averages, support, resistance, and market structure.

What makes PublicSentimentDash useful

  • It gives a fast read of financial media tone.
  • It tracks multiple markets in one place.
  • It separates public sentiment from technical direction.
  • It shows source votes, top headlines, market focus, and relevance classification.
  • It is designed as a free public market-awareness layer.

Important reminder

This website is not a trading system. It is not financial advice. It is a public-sentiment and market-awareness dashboard.

The best use is to combine sentiment with your own technical analysis, fundamental research, risk management, and independent judgment.
