About Public Sentiment Dash

Public Sentiment Dash is a public market sentiment dashboard built to organize financial headlines, public narratives, user votes, and market attention into a cleaner view.

It is designed for readers who follow stocks, indexes, forex, crypto, commodities, bonds, macro data, and financial news.

PSI
Public Sentiment Index for global market narrative.

What It Tracks

Public financial headlines, market topics, source votes, instrument sentiment, macro themes, and anonymous user voting.

Why It Exists

Markets move fast. This dashboard helps organize public sentiment so users can see what the market narrative is leaning toward.

How to Use It

Use it as context beside your own analysis. It is not a trading signal, recommendation, or financial advice.

Core Idea

Market Sentiment Is Context

Public narrative: what headlines and sources are emphasizing across markets.
Instrument view: sentiment broken down by indexes, currencies, crypto, commodities, bonds, and macro themes.
User voting: anonymous bullish and bearish votes add a public opinion layer.
Source coverage: multiple public financial sources are scanned to reduce dependence on one outlet.
Important

Not Financial Advice

Public Sentiment Dash is for informational and educational purposes only. It does not tell you what to buy, sell, short, hold, or trade.

Sentiment can be wrong, delayed, crowded, emotional, or incomplete. Always use your own judgment and risk management.
