About the project

About Public Sentiment Dash

Public Sentiment Dash is a free global market public sentiment dashboard built to help traders, investors, and market watchers quickly understand public financial narrative across major assets.

The platform organizes market headlines into a cleaner view of sentiment, source votes, market focus, relevant news, and technical direction.

Why This Exists

Markets move for many reasons: price action, fundamentals, central banks, earnings, economic data, commodities, geopolitical events, liquidity, and public attention.

Public Sentiment Dash focuses on one important layer: the public information layer. It helps show what financial sources are emphasizing, which themes are getting attention, and whether tone is leaning bullish, bearish, mixed, or neutral.

What It Covers

The dashboard is designed around a global all-markets view. It is not U.S.-only.

  • Indexes and futures
  • Bonds and rates
  • Forex and currencies
  • Crypto assets
  • Commodities
  • Macro themes such as Fed, CPI, jobs, GDP, inflation, tariffs, and geopolitics

What It Is Not

Public Sentiment Dash is not financial advice, not a trading signal, and not a replacement for independent research.

Public sentiment can be incomplete, delayed, misleading, or noisy. Use it as one layer of awareness, then confirm with price action, technicals, fundamentals, risk management, and trusted professional sources.
