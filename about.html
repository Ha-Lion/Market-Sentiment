About the project

About PublicSentimentDash

PublicSentimentDash is a free global market public sentiment dashboard built to help traders, investors, and market watchers quickly understand what public financial coverage is emphasizing across major assets.

The goal is simple: organize noisy financial headlines into a cleaner view of public narrative, source votes, market focus, relevant headlines, and technical direction.

Why This Exists

Markets move for many reasons. Price action, fundamentals, economic data, central banks, earnings, commodities, geopolitical events, and public attention can all matter.

PublicSentimentDash focuses on one important layer: the public information layer. It helps show what public financial sources are saying, which themes are getting attention, and whether the tone is leaning bullish, bearish, mixed, or neutral.

What It Covers

The dashboard is designed around a global all-markets view. It is not U.S.-only.

  • Indexes and futures
  • Bonds and rates
  • Forex and currencies
  • Crypto assets
  • Commodities
  • Macro themes such as Fed, CPI, jobs, GDP, inflation, tariffs, and geopolitics

What It Is Not

PublicSentimentDash is not financial advice, not a trading signal, and not a replacement for independent research.

Public sentiment can be incomplete, delayed, misleading, or noisy. Use it as one layer of market awareness, then confirm with price action, technicals, fundamentals, risk management, and trusted professional sources.
