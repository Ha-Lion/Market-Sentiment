Stock Market Guide

Stock Market Sentiment

Stock market sentiment is the public mood around equities, indexes, stock futures, volatility, earnings, interest rates, and risk appetite.

Public Sentiment Dash helps organize stock-related headlines so users can quickly see whether the public narrative is leaning bullish, bearish, mixed, or neutral.

Indexes

Tracks public narrative around S&P 500, Nasdaq, Dow, Russell, VIX, and global equity indexes.

Risk Appetite

Helps show when coverage is focused on fear, risk-on behavior, volatility, recession, earnings, or Fed policy.

Relevant Headlines

Filters market news into cleaner stock-related headlines for faster review.

What moves stock market sentiment?

Stock market sentiment can shift quickly when investors react to macro data, interest rates, inflation, earnings reports, central bank comments, geopolitical headlines, or sudden changes in volatility.
