Crypto Sentiment

Crypto sentiment is the public mood around Bitcoin, Ethereum, altcoins, regulation, ETF flows, blockchain adoption, exchange risk, liquidity, and broader risk appetite.

Public Sentiment Dash helps organize crypto-related headlines so users can quickly see whether the public narrative is leaning bullish, bearish, mixed, or neutral.

What Moves Crypto Sentiment?

Crypto sentiment often reacts to ETF news, regulation, exchange headlines, Bitcoin price momentum, liquidity conditions, interest-rate expectations, institutional flows, and risk appetite in broader markets.
